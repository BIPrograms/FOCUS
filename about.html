THE INTELLIGENCE STORY


 
 

Who are we? We're a growing analytics group within a Fortune 100 company. Big Data? No problem.

We've built relationships with the top IT manufacturers in the world. Through our success we saw the many B2B challenges and questions that are faced today. Are consumers making informed decisions for their specific needs? Are manufacturers in the right position to help customers succeed?

These questions gave birth to Intelligence: a personalized place anyone can get expert unbiased advice and make impactful decisions, Intelligently.
 

KNOWLEDGE IS POWER

 

We're building a community of collaboration; a place where seekers and experts can find a home to develop a knowledge base for professionals looking for straight-forward recommendations without the hard sell.

 

LOCAL IT EXPERTS


We have industry experts all across the country. Connect with local or national experts around your area through chat, phone, email or in person
